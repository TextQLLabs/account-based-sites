What technical background is required for this workshop?
The workshop is designed for participants with varying technical backgrounds. While data scientists and analysts will appreciate the technical details, business stakeholders will benefit from understanding the practical applications and business value. No coding experience is required.
How does TextQL's platform integrate with our existing data infrastructure?
TextQL works with your existing data infrastructure including AWS, Snowflake, Databricks, and BI tools like Tableau and Looker. It can be deployed on-premise within Capital One's environment, ensuring no sensitive data leaves your secure systems.

TextQL is SOC 2 Type II compliant and offers on-premise deployment options. The platform includes features for anonymizing sensitive data, role-based access control, and detailed audit logs. We'll discuss security in depth during the workshop.
